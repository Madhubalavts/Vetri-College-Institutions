ABOUT US

Accredited by NAAC with 'A++' Grade(Affiliated to University of XXX)

The Vetri College is a government-aided, Autonomous, college affiliated to the University of XXX. It was established in 2025. The college is re-accredited by NAAC with A++ (CGPA 3.61 out of 4) in the fourth cycle. The college envisions to promote educational and economic equity by helping aspiring and underprividged youth gain access to quality education and to nurture them into successful model citizens of the country. The vision is implemented through a 5E holistic development approach that strives to elevate, educate, empower, engage the students and enable them to excel in life and career. The institution considers Inclusivity, Student Centeredness, Excellence and Leadership Development as its core values.
